## OneNeo Bank — Concierge

> Scope, capabilities, and guardrails for the OneNeo Bank concierge demo.
> Company profile: [OneNeo-Bank](OneNeo-Bank.md).

### Purpose

The concierge is an in-app AI assistant that augments OneNeo Bank's existing rule-based
chatbot — covering more customer needs and reducing load on the limited human support desk.

> TODO: refine purpose and success criteria.

### Scope — the concierge should

- Answer product and account FAQs (deposits, lending, debit card, fees).
- Guide customers through common tasks (transfers, card usage, applications).
- Explain terms, rates, and procedures in plain language.

### Guardrails — the concierge must NOT

- Provide investment, tax, or legal advice.
- Move money or change account settings without proper authentication/authorization.
- Make definitive lending or eligibility decisions (defer to the screening flow).
