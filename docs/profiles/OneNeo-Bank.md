## OneNeo Bank

### Overview

`OneNeo Bank` is a fictional neobank (branchless digital bank) headquartered in Tokyo, Japan.
It was established in 2023 as a joint venture co-invested by a system integration (SI)
company and a real estate company. OneNeo Bank operates entirely through its mobile App —
there are no physical branches — and provides deposits, lending, and debit card services
to individual customers.

As of 2026, OneNeo Bank serves about 1 million customers in Japan.

**Mission:** make everyday banking effortless for individuals through a mobile-first,
app-only experience.

### Products & Services Overview

- **Deposits**
  - Ordinary deposit (普通預金) — the default account opened at onboarding.
  - Time deposit (定期預金) — fixed-term savings with a preferential interest rate.
  - Covered by Japan's deposit insurance system (預金保険) up to the statutory limit.
- **Lending**
  - Card loan (カードローン) — unsecured personal credit line with app-based screening.
  - Loan limits and interest rates are determined by an automated screening flow.
- **Debit card**
  - Issued with every account; usable online and in stores.
  - Point/cashback rewards on eligible spending.
  - Handles domestic and overseas (foreign-currency) transactions.

> Detailed product terms, rates, and fees: see [Products](Products.md).

### Channels & Support

- **Primary channel:** OneNeo Bank mobile App (iOS / Android). App-only; no branches.
- **In-app support:** a rule-based support chatbot built into the mobile App.
- **Human support:** outsourced to a 3rd-party call center — 5 FTE, 8 hours/day,
  5 days/week, excluding public holidays.

The concierge in this demo augments the existing rule-based chatbot, covering more
customer needs and reducing load on the limited human support desk.
